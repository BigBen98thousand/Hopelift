About Us

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the team behind HopeLift Foundation

Our Vision & Mission

Vision Statement

To create a society where every vulnerable person has equal access to opportunities, support, and sustainable livelihood.

Mission Statement

To empower widows, widowers, and the less privileged with financial support and skills, providing start-up grants for small businesses and offering emotional support for long-term stability and dignity.

Our Story

The story of HopeLift began in 2011 when our founder, during her NYSC service at Ilesha General Hospital, discovered her passion for extending care beyond clinical settings.

While serving in the hospital, she encountered patients who couldn't afford basic necessities. Guided by her nursing instinct, she began providing toiletries and essential supplies from her own resources.

Her service expanded to orphanage homes, where she committed 10% of her income monthly to purchase medications for children. Her December salary was dedicated to providing clothing for widows and food packages for struggling families.

In 2021, she relocated to Canada, continuing her mission by empowering widows and widowers with financial support to start small-scale businesses.

Our Impact

By 2025, her passion expanded into research on sustainable solutions, successfully setting up individuals with stable income sources through POS businesses and other ventures.

This led to the powerful realization: "Instead of giving people fish, I can help them learn to fish."

This became the seed of HopeLift Foundation â€” a platform built not just for temporary relief, but for long-term empowerment, independence, and transformation.

Today, HopeLift operates in Nigeria and Canada, with plans to expand to other regions where vulnerable communities need our support.

Our Programs

Comprehensive empowerment initiatives designed to create sustainable change

Start-Up Business Grants

We provide grants for petty trading, small businesses, and local income-generating ventures to help beneficiaries become self-sufficient. Our approach includes:

  • Micro-grants for small-scale businesses
  • Business planning assistance
  • Market linkage support
  • Ongoing mentorship

Skills Training Program

We offer training in various marketable skills to equip individuals for sustainable livelihoods:

  • Hairdressing and beauty therapy
  • Tailoring and fashion design
  • Baking and catering
  • Agricultural farming techniques
  • POS operations and small business management

Widows & Widowers Support

Our comprehensive support program for those who have lost their partners includes:

  • Monthly food packs and essential supplies
  • Emotional support and counseling
  • Regular health checks and medical assistance
  • Emergency welfare support
  • Community integration activities

Financial Literacy & Mentorship

We teach beneficiaries how to manage money effectively and grow their businesses:

  • Basic financial management skills
  • Savings and investment strategies
  • Credit management education
  • One-on-one business mentorship
  • Access to financial services

Youth Empowerment Bootcamp

Short intensive trainings for young people with business ideas but limited resources:

  • Entrepreneurship fundamentals
  • Digital skills training
  • Leadership development
  • Pitch competitions with seed funding
  • Post-training support and networking

Health & Wellness Program

Supporting the overall wellbeing of our beneficiaries through:

  • Regular health screening camps
  • Mental health support services
  • Health education workshops
  • Access to affordable healthcare
  • Nutritional support programs

Volunteer With Us

Our volunteers are the heart of HopeLift Foundation. Join our dedicated team and make a tangible difference in the lives of vulnerable individuals and communities.

As a HopeLift volunteer, you'll have the opportunity to:

  • Participate in community outreach programs
  • Mentor beneficiaries in our skills training programs
  • Assist with administrative and organizational tasks
  • Help with fundraising and awareness campaigns
  • Share your professional expertise in areas like business, health, or education

We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds and skill levels. Whether you can commit a few hours a week or participate in specific projects, your contribution matters.
