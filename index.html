Our Story

The Birth of HopeLift Empowerment Foundation

The story of HopeLift Empowerment Foundation began long before it had a name. In 2011, during her NYSC service year at Ilesha General Hospital, our founder discovered how deeply her passion for nursing could extend beyond the clinical space. As a nurse, caring for people came naturally to her, and this compassion became the foundation for everything that followed.

While serving in the hospital, she encountered patients who could not afford even the most basic items required for their stay. Guided by her nursing instinct to protect, comfort, and uplift, she began providing toiletries and essential supplies from her own resources. What started as simple acts of kindness soon evolved into a lifelong calling.

Her service expanded to orphanage homes, where she committed 10% of her income every month to purchase medications for children. Her December salary was dedicated to providing clothing for widows and sharing food packages with families struggling to meet their daily needs. Through these experiences, she learned that true care goes far beyond treatment — it is about restoring dignity, hope, and a sense of belonging.

In 2021, she relocated to Canada, opening a new chapter in her journey. With a broader perspective on global needs and opportunities, she continued her mission by empowering widows and widowers with financial support to start small-scale businesses. She also extended her outreach to vulnerable individuals and patients who needed assistance, fueled by the same compassionate spirit that shaped her years in nursing.

By 2025, her passion expanded into research on sustainable solutions for families and individuals in Nigeria. She discovered the potential of POS businesses and other small-scale ventures, successfully setting up three individuals with stable sources of income. That success sparked a powerful realization:

"Instead of giving people fish, I can help them learn to fish."

This revelation became the seed of HopeLift Empowerment Foundation — a platform built not just to provide temporary relief, but to create long-term empowerment, independence, and transformation. Her story reflects the heart of a nurse and a mother, the vision of a leader, and the dedication of a woman committed to uplifting communities. From a compassionate young nurse in Ilesha to the founder of an organization touching lives across continents, she continues to lead with empathy, integrity, and an unshakeable belief that every person deserves a chance to thrive, irrespective of their past.

Our Programs

Start-Up Business Grants

We provide financial grants for petty trading, small businesses, and local income-generating ventures. Our grants range from small seed funding for market stalls to larger investments for established small businesses looking to expand. Each grant comes with mentorship and business guidance to ensure sustainability.

  • Micro-grants for petty trading (₦100,000 - ₦200,000)
  • Small business grants (₦200,000 - ₦500,000)
  • Business expansion grants for existing businesses
  • Mentorship and business planning support

Skills Training Program

Our comprehensive skills training programs equip beneficiaries with practical, marketable skills for sustainable livelihoods. We partner with local experts and institutions to provide quality training in various fields.

  • Hairdressing and beauty therapy (3-month program)
  • Tailoring and fashion design (4-month program)
  • Baking and catering skills (2-month intensive)
  • POS operation and small business management
  • Basic farming techniques for sustainable agriculture

Widows & Widowers Support

We provide holistic support for widows and widowers facing economic and emotional challenges. Our program addresses immediate needs while working toward long-term stability.

  • Monthly food packs and essential supplies
  • Emotional support and counseling services
  • Health checks and medical assistance
  • Educational support for their children
  • Emergency welfare assistance

Financial Literacy & Mentorship

We teach beneficiaries how to manage money effectively, save consistently, and grow their businesses sustainably. Our mentorship program pairs beneficiaries with experienced business leaders.

  • Basic financial management workshops
  • Savings and investment strategies
  • Business planning and growth techniques
  • One-on-one mentorship matching
  • Regular progress tracking and support

Youth Empowerment Bootcamp

Our intensive bootcamps provide young people with business ideas but no funds the skills, knowledge, and connections they need to succeed. We focus on practical, actionable strategies for entrepreneurial success.

  • Idea validation and business model development
  • Digital skills and online business strategies
  • Pitching to potential investors
  • Access to our startup grant program
  • Ongoing support and networking opportunities

Be Part of Us

Why Volunteer With Us?

Join our community of change-makers and directly impact lives in vulnerable communities. Your time and skills can help restore dignity and rebuild lives.

  • Make a tangible difference in people's lives
  • Gain valuable experience in community development
  • Join a supportive network of like-minded individuals
  • Flexible volunteering opportunities to suit your schedule
  • Receive training and recognition for your contributions

Volunteer Roles

We have various volunteering opportunities based on your skills, interests, and availability:

  • Program Facilitators: Lead training sessions in your area of expertise
  • Mentors: Provide guidance and support to beneficiaries
  • Community Outreach: Help identify and connect with potential beneficiaries
  • Administrative Support: Assist with office tasks and program coordination
  • Fundraising Champions: Help organize events and raise awareness
